# JPIS Project Blueprint

JPIS is a modular legal intelligence platform for Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Its purpose is to connect laws, court decisions, legal literature, analytics, and AI-assisted legal research into one state-grade digital system.

## Repositories

- `ai.jpis.ba-front`
- `ai.jpis.ba-backend`
- `jpis-ingestion`
- `jpis-datasets`
- `jpis-docs`
